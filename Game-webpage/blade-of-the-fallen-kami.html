The Story

A World Broken
by Divine Wrath

Blade of the Fallen Kami is an action sword-fighting game set in a mythological samurai era. The player controls a lone warrior chosen by ancient spirits to restore balance in a world where gods — the Kami — have turned against humans. The game focuses on skill-based combat where timing and precision are more important than speed.

Instead of fast attacks, players must carefully watch enemy movements and use perfect parries to survive. Each enemy carries unique attack patterns, and mastering the parry system is the key to victory. The game rewards patience, focus, and strategy above all else.

"Only by surviving the gods' wrath can you hope to end it."

The Combat System

Every clash is a test of will. Read, wait, and strike — the perfect parry is your sword.

🛡

Parry-Based Combat

Defence is your primary weapon. Watch enemy stance, timing, and posture — then meet their blade at the exact moment of impact.

⚡

Perfect Timing Strikes

Land a flawless parry and the window opens. Unleash a divine counterattack that staggers even the mightiest Kami.

🔄

Adaptive Enemy Patterns

Enemies evolve. Patterns shift mid-battle as your foes learn your habits — no encounter plays out the same twice.

💀

High Difficulty, Pure Skill

No hand-holding, no shortcuts. Mastery is earned through failure, study, and relentless precision under pressure.
